Creating an engaging course

It is important to ensure that your course captures the attention of your participants in a well-structured, hands-on and rewarding learning experience. The most successful trainers spend time preparing the content for their course before they start recording it to ensure that their course helps participants achieve clear objectives.

Steps :

Step 1: Choose the topic for your course.
info

Choose an area where you are an expert and would really like to teach .

Step 2: Define your target participants and the learning objectives of your course

info

Before you start creating content for your course, it is important to determine who you are teaching and what your participants will learn or be able to accomplish at the end of your course. Find out more about questions to ask yourself about your participants and how to set clear learning goals.

Step 3: Determine how participants will apply what you teach.

info

Online learners like to benefit from hands-on learning: they want to apply what they have learned through exercises, activities, case studies and more. You can integrate many types of hands-on activities into your course to create an engaging online learning experience.

Step 4: Create your course frame.
info

The framework of your course is the foundation: you decide on the structure of the content of your course and how you organize it. If you prefer to use a predefined course frame, feel free to choose our 4MAT model.

Step 5: Starting building your course.
info

Once you have all the information you need, you can proceed with creating the sections and phases of your course one by one.
